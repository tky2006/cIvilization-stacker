📜 How to Play — Civilization Stacker

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1
Look at the civilization era you're building (e.g., Ancient Egypt or Classical Greece).
Step 2
Cards show different advancements: technology, agriculture, arts, and government.
Step 3
Drag cards to the stack area in chronological order — earliest at the bottom.
Step 4
Each correct placement builds the civilization higher. Wrong placement = stack shakes!
Step 5
Complete the full stack of 4–6 cards to advance to the next civilization!

⌨️ Keyboard Controls (Desktop)

1–5Select a card from the pool
↑ ↓Move selected card up/down in stack
EnterPlace selected card in next slot
BackspaceRemove last placed card
RReset current round
MMute / Unmute audio

👆 Touch Controls (Mobile & Tablet)

  • Drag cards from the left panel onto the stack area (right side)
  • Drop the card in a slot — it snaps if placed in the correct order
  • The stack builds from bottom (oldest) to top (newest)
  • Wrong placement shakes the stack — you lose a life!
  • Double-tap a card on the stack to remove it

💡 Game Tips

Think about what came first — agriculture or writing?
Civilizations need food before they can build cities!
Some cards depend on others — roads need the wheel.
Stack from OLDEST at the bottom to NEWEST at the top!
Watch for decoy cards — not everything belongs in this civilization!
Each civilization has its own unique timeline — trust your knowledge!
